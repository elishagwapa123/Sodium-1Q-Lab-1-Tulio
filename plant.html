My Favorite Plant

Pink Tulips



Tulips are vibrant, cup-shaped flowers belonging to the genus Tulipa, known for their wide variety of colors and forms,

making them one of the most popular spring-blooming flowers worldwide. Tulips flowers grow from bulbs. They usually bloom in spring.

Carolus Clusius first identified Viral Infection tulip known as broken tulip. These plants are perennial which means they do not contain woody structure, also live for more than two years.
